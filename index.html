Tomfoolery Demos

Overview

Tomfoolery Demos (stylized as "Tomfoolery_Demos") is a collection of demos of many songs by Melody McGinley that would later appear on her debut album, [ALBUM].

Out of 13 CDs, 11 were to be sold by Melody at Sonic Lunch 2024 for $6, however some recipients received one after simply asking for one. The remaining 2 were given to Andrew Horowitz and Matthew Altruda.

Melody has called the release "unproffesional", stating that it was poorly planned and involved many last minute decisions. Melody and her dad stayed up late the night before she would leave for the event, burning each CD one-by-one, which led to one of the CDs being mistakenly placed in the finished pile before being burned.
